About Me

I am a postdoctoral research associate in the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science at Mount Allison University. I am also an associate lecturer in the Mathematics Department at the University of Massachusetts-Boston.

My research is in higher category theory, specifically, double categories and their applications. I am also interested in 2-categories, toposes, and internal logic.
